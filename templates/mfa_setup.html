Frequently Asked Questions

What is TOTP?

TOTP (Time-based One-Time Password) is a time-synchronized code that changes every 30 seconds. Your authenticator app generates these codes based on a shared secret.

What authenticator apps can I use?

Popular options include: Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, Authy, FreeOTP, or 1Password. Any TOTP-compatible app will work.

What are backup codes?

Backup codes are emergency one-time passwords. If you lose your authenticator or phone, you can use these codes to access your account. Each one can only be used once, so store them safely.

What if I lose my authenticator?

Use one of your backup codes to log in. Then, disable MFA and set it up again with a new device. Contact an administrator if you've lost both your authenticator and backup codes.
